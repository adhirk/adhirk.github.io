Flights to Vancouver

Vancouver is served by Vancouver International Airport (YVR), with direct flights from major cities worldwide.

Public Transport

Vancouver offers a wide range of public transportation, including buses, SkyTrain, and ferries.

Driving

If you're coming from nearby cities like Seattle or Whistler, driving is a great way to explore the scenic landscapes along the way.
